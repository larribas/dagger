    AsPickle¶

    AsPickle serializes values to and from Python's Pickle protocol.

    ⛔ Limitations¶

    The Pickle protocol has no limitations as far as serializing native Python objects goes.

    Nevertheless, we strongly recommend you read the warnings in the official documentation, and the comparison with other kinds of serializers to understand the behavior and the dangers of pickle serialization.
